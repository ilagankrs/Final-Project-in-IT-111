What is Gender Equality?

Gender equality is a common human right that needs to be served to anyone. It is important to communities, especially to human beings as themselves.

It is fundamental human rights to expand to humans full potential to have its full potential development to themselves and for their growth. It will empower their productivity and economic growth as they fly their wings outside of their comfort zone without hesitation of being discriminated and judged by others. Furthermore, the significance of gender equality will not apply to women and men, it applies to all genders. It discusses that all genders are applicable to any task; it may be men work, women can also do that such things and vice versa, men can also do also women work.

By Having Gender Equality...

Gender equality is simply as the equal rights and opportunities to any gender. It is not only women are greatly affected by this, it affects all aspect of gender especially to the LGBTQIA+ Community that are mostly being discriminated by others. This plays a significant impact to their selves as they lose some of their self-confidence, self-esteem, etc., that may also affect their mental health by being discriminated for who they are or what gender they are by not having equality to all genders.
